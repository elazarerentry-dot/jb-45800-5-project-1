📊 About the System

Expense Manager is a personal finance web application built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It lets you easily track, manage, and analyze your daily expenses — all saved directly in your browser with no server needed.

  • 🏠 Home — Add, edit, and delete expenses with full validation
  • 🔍 Filter — Search expenses by year, month, day, or amount range
  • 📊 Charts — Visualize spending with a pie chart (by category) and bar chart (by year)
  • ⬇ Export — Download all your data as a CSV or PDF report

All data is stored in localStorage, so your expenses are saved between visits without any login or database.
